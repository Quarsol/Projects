23 February, 2018

Today I plan on doing a few simple things.
1. Finish drill 3 of building simple websites.
2. Finish drill 2 of Learn 2 Learn.
3. Finish this short term goal drill.

Reflection:

It took me a little longer than intended, but I did manage to complete the goals I had set out. I feel like my clearing of the stage made things go by pretty well, as there were less distractions on my screen to pull me away from what I was doing. The break schedule I set for myself also went fairly well I feel. I never sat and stared at the screen too long, and if I felt myself starting to, Id get up and walk around or go stretch or something to get away from the screen for a few minutes.

As for what didn't go well, I think my ambition was a little too high. I assumed that since I was beginning to get the hang of it and work faster with HTML, and especially CSS, that I could get everything done within a single day. I got most of the way there, but a few unforseen things snagged me for longer than I thought they would have, and thus my 1 day goal turned into more of a 2 day goal. Still managed to get everything done, just not in the time frame I had imagined. I will need to work on that in the future, ensuring that my goals are in a more reasonable time frame for my current skill level. Better to expect it to take more time than less.

Next time, I will ensure that I measure my competency better before I set a goal that I think is easily achieveable, and proves otherwise. Should never overestimate your ability.
