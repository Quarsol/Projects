About Me

What to say about myself. Well, I am a former member of the US Army, I love geek and nerd culture such as video games and table top games, and I have a fondness for coding. I first got a taste of it a few years back when I watched a Youtuber participate in Ludum Dare 48 hour game making jam. I was heavily intrigued by his use of Unity to make a game from just his thoughts. From there, I began watching some of his content on programming in general. I found it highly facscinating. I didn't think to pursue it much at the time as I was focused on other things in regards to my work life. I slowly began to think more and more that I needed a change of pace. I needed some form of work that I actually enjoyed and didn't slog through for 8 hours just for a paycheck. It was at that point I heard about Galvanize and decided to give it a shot. From here, we will look at some of the things that I have learned and continue to strive for while attending this program.
